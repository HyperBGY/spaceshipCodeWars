A group of durable cockroach-human hybrids travels to Kepler-22b, their exoskeletons glinting in the ship’s light.

Adapted to harsh conditions, they monitor unusual signals and energy patterns, searching for signs of life while embodying Earth’s resilience.

CAPTAIN

Alex Morgan

Alex Morgan commands the mission with unwavering focus and a calm presence under pressure. Experienced in both strategy and leadership, Alex makes critical decisions for the safety and success of the crew, balancing risk with opportunity while navigating the vast unknown of deep space.
